Problem Definition
Master‑Apprentice ≠ Scalable
Encode tacit know‑how as a governed, shared Ontology

Foundry’s ONTOLOGY transforms individual mental models into computable objects, relationships, lineage links, and governed writebacks. Knowledge becomes a continuously updated, permissioned graph—interrogable, auditable, and reusable—so capability compounds independent of staffing bottlenecks.

"Scaling performance now depends on how fast the organization encodes and reuses its operational reasoning—not on how quickly expertise can be relayed person to person."
